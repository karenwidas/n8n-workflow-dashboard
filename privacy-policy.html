Privacy Policy — IMG Services FM Invoicing App

Last updated: March 4, 2026

This application ("FM Invoicing") is an internal tool developed and used exclusively by IMG Services. It is not distributed to the public.

Data Collected

This app accesses QuickBooks Online account data (customers, invoices) solely to automate invoice creation for internal business operations.

Data Use

Data accessed through this app is used only to create and send invoices on behalf of IMG Services. No data is sold, shared, or transmitted to third parties.

Data Storage

OAuth tokens required to authenticate with QuickBooks Online are stored securely within the n8n automation platform. No financial data is stored outside of QuickBooks Online.

Data Retention

Authentication credentials are retained only as long as necessary to operate the app. They may be revoked at any time through the QuickBooks Online account settings.
